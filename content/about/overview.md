## Me
I’m a Senior SRE at NBCUniversal, currently within Customer Product Security.

I started my career on a school helpdesk where I learned of my need to automate the mundane stuff (PowerShell being my main tool then... I know). If you don't automate, you never get to do the interesting work. Since then, I’ve moved from building full multi-service platforms in GCP with Kubernetes at Sky to designing, building, and maintaining high-availability, low-latency security tooling on AWS to support multiple propositions around the world.

### What I've actually built
**Infrastructure**: Designed, led, and built a GCP multi-service platform and "serverless-first" AWS environments.

**Data Pipelines**: Built ETL pipelines in AWS Glue (Spark) with strict zero-loss, high-uptime requirements. Along with an accompanying Kafka topic schema sampling service to help end users understand the data they will be recieving.

**Security**: Developed an internal self-service platform for pentesters and built automated cloud asset visibility tools to track deployed resources through the service lifecycle.

**Automation**: I still feel the urge to automate just about everything within my power. Be it patching something, checking open PRs, or scaling down to save costs.
